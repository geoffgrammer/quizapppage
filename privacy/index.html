Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Overview

While You Wait (“we,” “our,” “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Diagnostics & analytics (if enabled): Crash logs and performance metrics used to improve stability.
  • Support communications: If you email us, we receive your email and message content.

How we use information

To operate, maintain, and improve the app; respond to support; and detect/prevent abuse. We do not sell your data.

Data retention

Diagnostic data is kept only as long as necessary for troubleshooting and improvement, then deleted or anonymized.

Your choices

  • Opt-out: You can disable analytics in iOS Settings if used (Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements).
  • Access/Deletion: Email support@whileyouwait.com with “Privacy Request.” We’ll respond within 30 days.

Contact

Questions? support@whileyouwait.com
